TF2 Asymmetric TF2 Map


Built for 6v6 gameplay, the size of the map and the central bloodbath makes it action packed. The spawn placements make it easy to get back to the turmoil, while not being on the critical path. There are multiple entrances to both bases, providing different ways to get to the objective and return. 

The playtesting showed that the map is very balanced, intuitive, adapted to the different roles and simply a lot of fun. The early sketchup mockup had a bloodbath too big with almost no covers. Fixing it was my priority and the result is that there's now enough covers for interesting gameplay with snipers, while also being able to stay in close range with scouts.
