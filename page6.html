The Rift

Doom Single Player Map

2.5 minutes

The goal was for the level to be 2.5 minutes walkthrough without any enemies or ingredients.

DLC

Made as a DLC, with the player having knowledge of the game

Design Intentions :
-Campaign style single player level
-Weapon progression and increasing difficulty
-Fluid use of multiple environments (industrial, lab and hell)
-Interesting well timed events and triggers


Challenges :
-Very limited resources and memory pool
-Item and demons progression in such a short level
-Creating my vision of the level using mostly premade layout blocks
