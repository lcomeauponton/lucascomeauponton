2 minutes

DLC

Design Intentions :
-One single room with 2 minutes of content, rather than a linear experience of multiple rooms
-Backtracking and use of the same ingredients more than once

Challenges :
-Having many ingredients and links between them in a small room without too much clutter
-Keeping a proper framing at all times and guide the player in the puzzle
